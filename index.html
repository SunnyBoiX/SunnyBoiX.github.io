About NHS Hack Club

We're a community of passionate coders and creators at Northville High School

Building the Future, One Line of Code at a Time

NHS Hack Club is Northville High School's student-run programming club where students of all skill levels come together to learn, create, and innovate.

Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced developer, you'll find a welcoming community where you can grow your skills, work on exciting projects, and prepare for a future in technology

We believe in learning by doing, collaboration over competition, and creating technology that makes a difference.

What We Do
